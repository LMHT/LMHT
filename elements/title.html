<title>

English Base Word: Title
Spanish Word Equivalent:

Definición

Definition

The <title> tag is a name or descriptive caption given to the document. This will show in the browser's title bar and the tab for the page.

¿Cómo recordar?

How to Remember

While you can’t read a book by it’s cover, you can get a pretty good idea what it’s about by the title. A <title> is a quick way of identifying what the document is about.


    <html>
      <head>
        <title>Document title</title>
      </head>
    </html>
