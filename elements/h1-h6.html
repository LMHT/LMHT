<h1-h6>

English Base: Header
Spanish translation/Traducción en Español:

Definición

Definition

Not to be confused with the <header> element which is a whole section of a web page; heading elements are used to describe the topic or give the title of the section it is introducing. There are six graduated levels of font size and boldness with <h1> being the largest and boldest down to <h6> as the smallest and least boldface.

¿Cómo recordar?

How to Remember

In a concert, the number one artist headlining in the show is the biggest and brightest. The second in importance is slightly smaller and under the headliner, and so on down the line. Just as heading level 1 <h1> is the biggest and boldest, heading level 2 <h2> is slightly smaller and less bold.

    
      <h1>William Shakespeare</h1>
      <h2>William Shakespeare's Biography</h2>
      <h2>The Works of William Shakespeare</h2>
      <h3>Shakespeare's Poems and Sonnets</h3>
      <h3>Shakespeare's Plays</h3>
      <h4>Romeo and Juliet</h4>
      <h5>Act I</h5>
      <h5>Act II</h5>
      <h6>Act II, Scene 1</h6>
