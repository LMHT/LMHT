<i>

English Base: Italic Text
Spanish translation/Traducción en Español:

Definición

Definition

The <i> element is used to indicate text that needs to stand apart from its surrounding text. The most common uses are: technical terms, foreign language, conversation or thoughts of characters in stories or just as a way to add stress to a particular word or text.

¿Cómo recordar?

How to Remember

I is for interesting as well as italic and the <i> element is used to make interesting things stand apart by italicizing them.
