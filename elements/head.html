<head>

English Base Word: Head
Spanish Word Equivalent:

Definición

Definition

The <head> tag contains metadata. Metadata is general information about a document, such as the title, keywords and author.

¿Cómo recordar?

How to Remember

Just like the head of the body is at the top, the head of the document is also at the top.

    
      <html>
        <head>
          <title>Document title</title>
        </head>
      </html>
