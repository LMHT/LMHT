<strong>

English Base: Strong emphasis
Spanish translation/Traducción en Español:

Definición

Definition

Element used to indicate importance of selected text usually by putting it in bold print. <b> boldface can also be used, however <strong> is considered a logical state and <b> bold is a physical state. In CSS, it is more logical to assign attributes to <strong> text to separate it and show emphasis, including assigning the physical attribute of being <b> boldface text as well as other attributes such as font-size or font-color.

¿Cómo recordar?

How to Remember

Text in boldface shows strong emotion as its darker and bolder than its surrounding text.
