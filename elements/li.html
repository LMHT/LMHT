<li>

English Base: List Item
Spanish translation/Traducción en Español:

Definición

Definition

A child element that is an item in a list. The <li> must be in a parent container either: an ordered list <ol>, unordered list <ul>, or a menu <menu>. In menus and unordered lists, each list item is usually separated by bullet points and in ordered lists they are separated in ascending or descending order using letters or numbers.

¿Cómo recordar?

How to Remember

A list item can be as simple as an item on a grocery list or as complicated as a step in directions on how to build a website.

    
      <ol start= "1">
        <li>Heat a cup of milk on the stove</li>
        <li>Put it in a mug</li>
        <li>Add 3 heaping tablespoons of cocoa mix</li>
      </ol>
