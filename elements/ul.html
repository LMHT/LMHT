Spanish translation/Traducción en Español:

Definición

Definition

Used to group a list of items where the order that they are in is not important. The bullet style can be changed to a wide variety of objects from dots to dashes to squares or even pictures.

¿Cómo recordar?

How to Remember

Unordered lists will start with bullets as they are not in order and have no numbering system to keep them in a specific order.
