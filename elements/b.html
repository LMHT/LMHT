Definición

Definition

Originally, <b> was meant to make text boldface. Currently, it is used to indicate a section of text that is styled differently from other text. The <b> element is usually used when other command codes (<em>, <strong>, etc) don't work well for the situation. It is also used to indicate keywords, product names, and other text that need to stand out but do not have any emphasis on the text.
